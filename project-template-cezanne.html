Research

Research Goals:

- What areas of the system are users focusing on most often (per role).

- Validate current assumptions and break common myths.

- How do users interpret our navigation currently (qualitative/quantitative).

- Discover common themes and interaction patterns through feedback and analytics.

- Gain insights into how users organise our navigation from their perspective and how difficult the learnability of new designs could be.

- Conformation of the visual identity and efficiency of the new navigation.

- Build/update personas from the feedback for future reference.

- Expand the scope of our research.
